Our Story

“गजाली” means joyful conversations

…and to make those conversations even more flavorful, we have Malvani Gajali.

Where every dish reflects the culture of Malvan and the magic of authentic seafood.

The dream and inspiration behind this place is Chef Nilesh Sawant — a son of Malvan, who gave the taste of the seashore an unforgettable form with his own spices.

For whom? For our Dombivlikars.

Here, every platter holds the moistness of Konkan, the tempering of spices, and homely warmth.

At Malvani Gajali, it’s not just a meal — it’s the taste of Malvan and the joy of conversations.

The Owner
The Dream Behind the Plate

Chef Nilesh Sawant

Chef Nilesh Samant is a professional chef and avid culinary enthusiast with a rich and long experience in the hospitality industry. He has honed his skills by working on various exciting projects for over a decade.

When he is out of the kitchen, he constantly researches new recipes, food cultures and fusions, which always brings innovation and creativity to his work. Although he is well-versed in all types of food cultures, his love for the authentic Malvani cuisine of his soil is the inspiration behind the concept of ‘Malvani Ghajali’.
